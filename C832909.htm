Card 1
  1. What do you think of the weather today? How would you compare it to the past week or month?
  2. How was the traffic the last time you drove or walked somewhere?
  3. Describe the last meal you ate.
  4. What was the last restaurant you went to? How was the service there?
  5. What do you think of the color of the room you’re currently in?

Card 2
  1. Tell me about one of your favorite foods.
  2. What holiday do you enjoy most and why?
  3. Tell me about a favorite movie.
  4. What did you do last weekend?
  5. What is your favorite color?

Card 3
  1. Are there any sports or other physical activities you enjoy?
  2. What are examples of the types of clothes you like to wear?
  3. What is something you like to do in your free time?
  4. What is your favorite season and why?
  5. What kind of music do you like to listen to?

Card 4
  1. What is the earliest memory you recall from childhood?
  2. Do you dream while asleep, and if so, do you remember your dreams?
  3. Do you prefer savory or sweet foods? Why?
  4. Do you have any pets? If so, will you tell me about them?
  5. Do you have siblings? If so, will you tell me about them?

Card 5
  1. What kind of television shows do you enjoy?
  2. When was your last birthday? Did you celebrate and how?
  3. Do you have a favorite book? Why?
  4. Is there a celebrity or public figure that you admire or look up to?
  5. Do you attend school currently? If so, what do you like or not like about it?

Card 6
  1. Do you work a job currently? If so, what do you like or not like about it?
  2. Do you enjoy traveling? What do you like or dislike most about it?
  3. Are there things or skills you believe you are especially good at?
  4. What fun activities have you not tried yet, but you would like to try one day?
  5. Do you have any pet peeves?

Card 1
  1. What do you think of the weather today? How would you compare it to the past week or month?
  2. How was the traffic the last time you drove or walked somewhere?
  3. Describe the last meal you ate.
  4. What was the last restaurant you went to? How was the service there?
  5. What do you think of the color of the room you’re currently in?

Card 2
  1. What is an academic or career goal you have for yourself for the next year? The next five years?
  2. What is a personal goal you have for yourself for the next year? The next five years?
  3. What is a hobby, activity, or interest you enjoy that most other people also enjoy?
  4. What is a political, philosophical, or social opinion you have that I would probably agree with?
  5. What might be a personal preference we share that we haven’t discussed before?

Card 3
  1. What is a hobby, activity, or interest you enjoy that most other people do not enjoy?
  2. What is a hobby, activity, or interest you do not enjoy that most other people enjoy?
  3. What is a political, philosophical, or social opinion you have that I would probably not agree with?
  4. What might be a personal preference we do not share that we haven’t discussed before?
  5. What are your true thoughts/feelings on a mutual friend or acquaintance we share?

Card 4
  1. What is a personal relationship you have or have had that did not meet your expectations?
  2. When was the last time someone disappointed you? When was the last time someone made you happy?
  3. Is there something about you that your friends don’t know, or an experience that you feel alone with?
  4. What do you like the most about spending time with me? What do you dislike the most?
  5. Do you have an opinion or judgement about me you might be afraid to tell me?

Card 5
  1. What might be a weakness you have that you rarely tell others about?
  2. Have you done something that you’ve been too embarrassed or afraid to tell anyone about?
  3. What do you doubt most about yourself?
  4. What do you truly think about our relationship?
  5. Do you want us to spend more time together? Why or why not?

Card 6
  1. How much do you care about me and why?
  2. What would you be willing to sacrifice for our relationship?
  3. Tell me what makes you feel most vulnerable about our relationship.
  4. Do you have any emotions or desires for our relationship you may not have expressed before?
  5. What do you want our relationship to be in the future?
